Q.8 How can i create my own Notebook?

You can create your Notebook by clicking "Create Notebook" button appearing in top blue header. Thenafter a Notebook form will appear for subject name, date, monetary advantage, innovation, investment, visible and hidden. 'Subject name' is any specific name chosen by you to appear on notebook cover. 'Date' is a calender for chosing any date of this notebook's creation. 'Monetary advantage' button is for getting money into your account. Choose 'Innovation' radio button if you are going to innovate inside notebook(it will appear as green circle at right top corner of notebook cover in homepage after notebook creation) or choose 'Investment' if you are going to create notebook about investment inside notebook(it will appear as orange circle at bottom right corner of notebook cover in homepage after notebook creation) . Radio button 'none' indicates that neither it is about innovation nor investment. Choose 'visible' if you want this notebook to let visible to other users. Choose 'Hidden' for hiding this notebook from other users.Fill notebook form then submit. After you submitted notebook successfully you can use 'Add page as image', 'Add page as video', 'Add page as text', 'Add page as audio' for adding image/video /text/audio into your notebook page.

Q. 12 How can i 'Create Advertisement?

You can create advertisement after clicking ' Create ad' appearing in drop down list just below profile name in header. Click 'Create ad' then you will find 'Ad Creation Form'.fill this form. Add Image by choosing 'single pic', 'multiple pic', 'audio clip', 'video clip' through browse. Write in textbox for 'description Headline for add', 'Feature', 'Discription'. Fill email address inside 'Advertiser's email id. Put mobile number inside Advertiser's mobile number. Fill amount inside' Price of Product/service'. Fill website name inside 'Website/URL address. Now the next form is' Target user information '. Here Choose desired country from dropdown list of ' country' where you want to publish your ad. In the same way Choose 'state' and 'city' where you want to publish your advertisement. 'Start date' is when you want your advertisement start to appear. 'End date' is when you want your advertisement to end. 'Check availability ' is for checking availability of space for advertisement during chosen date interval. ' Amount per day ' is showing amount to be paid by you for displaying advertisement in every page of notebook. Number of days is showing total number of days for which you are going to display your advertisement. Total amount is the amount which you are going to pay for displaying this advertisement. Click 'Place order' button for placing order. After clicking 'Place order' button you will get 'Billing' form. Fill all required information about billing in 'Billing' form. Submit billing form. Thenafter transaction will take place. Now your advertisement will start to display inside every page of notebook of chosen city, state, country by you.
